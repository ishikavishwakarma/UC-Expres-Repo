Our Services

We provide a wide range of custom-made courier services for all your consignments, whether it is a Document, non-document, high value, urgent, or vital shipments both in India or Internationally.

Domestic Services

UC Express is capable of delivering time sensitive packages within a span of 24-48 hrs all across India. Till now we have made 75,000+ deliveries over 4000 Cities and over 19097 Pin codes. For your domestic courier needs, it offers a wide variety of logistical solutions that are both tailored to your demands and economical.

International Services

It offers you a cost-effective and efficient International Shipping cargo services. With its fast and reliable transport of Doc or Non-Doc parcels to more than over 150 countries with delivery within 2-5 days.

Priority Services

UC Express has become the leader in providing package and document services quickly enough for your clients to get them the following business day. This package is designed exclusively for time-sensitive documents and packages.

Premium Express Cargo

A specialized and convenient service designed for transportation & distribution of commercial consignments.You can expect your cargo to be delivered within 24-72 hours with the help of this organized delivery process within the country

Supply Chain Solutions

UC Express provides supply chain solutions to fulfill your B2B needs. From collecting the package to warehousing, transportation to last mile delivery we can support your need with our tailor made supply chain solutions as per your needs.

International Freight

A tailor-made solution for all your International couriers. UC Express can Ship all your International consignments from Dox,Non-Dox to Bulk transportation at a very reasonable cost in comparison to the current market.

Freight On Delivery

With this service, the shipper can send a parcel for free because the recipient will be responsible to pay the shipping charges. At the time of delivery, the recipient will also be billed the standard shipping costs in addition to a small fee for the Freight on Delivery service.

Cash On Delivery

In this process, the payment is collected by the courier delivery guy at the time of delivery from the recipient and remits it to the sender.

Free Door Step Pick-Up

UC expresses also provides free doorstep parcel pick-up service for all your Dox and Non-Dox couriers.
